### Q: What if clients compare us to cheaper options?

**A:** This is why you compete on value, not price. Show ROI, demonstrate speed/quality differences, share success stories. Clients who choose purely on price aren't ideal clients anyway.

### Q: How do we justify the higher prices?

**A:** You don't justify the price‚Äîyou demonstrate the value. Calculate time/money saved, show ROI timeline, present case studies. Make it clear this is an investment that pays for itself.
