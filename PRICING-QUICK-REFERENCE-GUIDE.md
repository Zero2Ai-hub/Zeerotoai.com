## ðŸŽ“ PRICING PSYCHOLOGY TIPS

### Use These Tactics:

1. **Anchor High**
   - Show enterprise tier first
   - Makes other tiers seem affordable
   - Example: "$50K enterprise... or our popular $8K growth tier"

2. **Charm Pricing**
   - $4,997 not $5,000
   - $9,950 not $10,000
   - Feels significantly cheaper psychologically

3. **Decoy Effect**
   - Show 3 options
   - Make middle one most attractive (price + value)
   - Example: $3K (basic) | $6K (best value!) | $12K (premium)

4. **Price Per Day**
   - "$8,000" sounds expensive
   - "$22/day for complete automation" sounds cheap
   - Same price, different framing

5. **Cost Comparison**
   - "Less than hiring one employee"
   - "Cost of 2 weeks of current process"
   - "Price of 10 hours of manual work per month"
